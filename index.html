Overlooking Falmouth’s historic harbour, a one minute level stroll into town, The Grove Hotel lies in a prime location. Behind the grand Georgian facade are 17 smartly finished en-suite bedrooms blending classic architecture with modern stylish furnishings.

Our Premier rooms offer views over the bustling harbour and the award winning museum complex, home to Rick Steins newest fish restaurant. Close to station, ferries, buses and Falmouth’s best restaurants

N.B.There is public parking all around but we do not have a designated hotel car park.
